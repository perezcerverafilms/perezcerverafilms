Bendita Rebeldía (2020)

Bendita Rebeldía (Acidental Blessings) is the debut feature from Laura Pérez Cervera — a sharp, heartfelt comedy about a young woman forced to return home after divorce, debt, and dreams gone sideways. What she finds is a second chance wrapped in chaos, rebellion, and a few unexpected blessings.

Available on: Amazon Prime, Apple TV, YouTube Movies, Roku and more.
