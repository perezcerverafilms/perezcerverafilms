Laura Pérez Cervera

Laura Pérez Cervera is a screenwriter and filmmaker who tells bold, heartfelt stories with soul and a little bit of sparkle.

She’s the writer-director behind Bendita Rebeldía, a feature film praised for its strong female lead and emotional bite, and was part of the Creative Direction team behind Disney’s Oscar-winning Encanto—yes, that magical family.

Whether it’s grounded drama, a dash of magical realism, or something totally offbeat, Laura brings sharp storytelling, big heart, and vibrant visuals to everything she does. Her work spans drama, comedy, and the fantastical, always grounded in deep empathy and a strong sense of place.
