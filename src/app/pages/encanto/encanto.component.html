Encanto (2021)

Writer and director at Walt Disney Animation Studios from 2021 to 2022.

Part of the creative leadership team behind Encanto, the Academy Award-winning animated film by WDAS. Collaborated with producers Yvett Merino, Jennifer Lee, Brad Simonsen, and Clack Spenser, alongside directors and writers Jared Bush, Byron Howard, and Charise Castro Smith.

Available on Disney+
